1. Player: Virat Kohli
   - Team: India
   - Position: Batsman

Virat Kohli is an Indian cricketer and often regarded as one of the best batsmen in the world. He is known for his aggressive and technically sound batting style. Virat Kohli has achieved numerous records in international cricket and has been the captain of the Indian cricket team in all three formats. He has led India to significant victories in various series and tournaments.

2. Player: Steve Smith
   - Team: Australia
   - Position: Batsman

Steve Smith is an Australian cricketer known for his unorthodox batting technique and ability to score crucial runs for his team. He has been a key player for the Australian national cricket team and has held the position of captain as well. Steve Smith has been considered as one of the top batsmen in the world and has a remarkable record in Test cricket.

3. Player: Kane Williamson
   - Team: New Zealand
   - Position: Batsman

Kane Williamson is a New Zealand cricketer who is known for his elegant batting style and calm demeanor on the field. He has been the captain of the New Zealand cricket team across all formats and has led the team to significant success. Kane Williamson is highly regarded for his consistency in scoring runs and has been one of the key players for New Zealand in international cricket.

4. Player: Ben Stokes
   - Team: England
   - Position: All-rounder

Ben Stokes is an English cricketer known for his versatile skills as an all-rounder. He is capable of contributing with both bat and ball, making him a valuable asset to the England cricket team. Ben Stokes has played crucial roles in England's victories, including the 2019 ICC Cricket World Cup where he played a match-winning innings in the final.

5. Player: Rashid Khan
   - Team: Afghanistan
   - Position: Bowler

Rashid Khan is an Afghan cricketer who is considered one of the best spin bowlers in the world. Known for his deceptive leg-spin and ability to pick up wickets regularly, Rashid Khan has made a significant impact in international cricket. He has been a key player for the Afghanistan national team and has played in various T20 leagues around the world, showcasing his talent as a skilled bowler.
